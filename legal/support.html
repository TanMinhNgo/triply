Frequently asked questions

How does Triply create my itinerary?

You tell Triply your destination, dates, number of travelers, budget level, interests, and pace. The app uses AI to generate a suggested day-by-day itinerary with places to visit, hotel ideas, approximate map locations, and an estimated per-person budget.

Are the suggestions and prices accurate?

Itineraries, place and hotel suggestions, map pins, and budgets are AI-generated estimates and can be inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date. Always verify opening hours, availability, pricing, and entry requirements independently before you rely on them. Triply is a planning and inspiration tool — not a booking service or travel agency.

Does Triply book flights or hotels for me?

No. Triply helps you plan and organize a trip, but it does not book, reserve, or pay for flights, accommodation, or tours. Any booking you make is directly with the relevant provider.

How do I sign in?

You sign in with your Google or Apple account through our authentication provider, Clerk. Triply never creates or stores a password for you.

How do I delete a trip or my account?

You can delete any trip from within the app. To remove everything, open the profile screen and delete your account — this permanently deletes your account and associated data, including your trips, itineraries, and chats, and cannot be undone.

Is Triply free?

Yes. Triply is currently free to use, and the app does not process payments or offer in-app purchases. To keep the service available, some usage limits apply (for example, a cap on how many trips you can generate per day).
